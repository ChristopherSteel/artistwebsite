Christopher Steel

About

Christopher Steel (1968) has exhibited still and moving image work in galleries and festivals internationally for almost 30 years. His work is held in the collections of LUX (London), Light Cone (Paris) and Caixa Forum (Barcelona). He lives and works in London.
