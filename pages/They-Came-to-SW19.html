They Came to SW19

2017, 16mm, color, sound, single screen, 6' 30

Statement

A moving portrait of the queue for the Wimbledon tennis championships. The background spectacle moves into the foreground and onto a queue of film-frames passing through the camera. I filmed from a bicycle along the line of the longest recreational queue in Europe. I grew up nearby and found this background preamble a bigger spectacle than the main event. The camera tracks alongside the queue, frames stutter through the camera paralleling the shuffling individuals. They make a mile-long performance, held together by a ribbon and politeness.
